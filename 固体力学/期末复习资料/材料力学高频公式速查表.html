Formula Sheet

材料力学高频公式速查表

这一版是浏览器公式渲染版，适合临考前快速翻看。

1. 内力与内力图

  • 截面法：先取分离体，再列平衡，求 \(N, Q, M, T\)
  • \(\dfrac{dQ}{dx}=-q(x)\)，\(\dfrac{dM}{dx}=Q(x)\)
  • 集中力使 \(Q\) 跳跃；集中力偶使 \(M\) 跳跃；\(Q=0\) 常对应 \(M\) 极值点

2. 拉压、温度、薄壁圆筒

  • \(\sigma=\dfrac{N}{A}\)
  • \(\varepsilon=\dfrac{\Delta l}{l}\)
  • \(\Delta l=\int \dfrac{N}{EA}\,dx\)
  • \(\varepsilon_T=\alpha \Delta T\)，\(\Delta l_T=\alpha\Delta T\,l\)
  • \(\sigma_\theta=\dfrac{pD}{2t}\)，\(\sigma_z=\dfrac{pD}{4t}\)

3. 扭转、剪切、连接件

  • \(\tau=\dfrac{Tr}{J_p}\)，\(\tau_{\max}=\dfrac{TR}{J_p}\)
  • \(\varphi=\int\dfrac{T}{GJ_p}\,dx\)
  • 实心圆 \(J_p=\dfrac{\pi d^4}{32}\)，空心圆 \(J_p=\dfrac{\pi(D^4-d^4)}{32}\)
  • \(\tau=\dfrac{VQ}{Ib}\)
  • \(\tau_{\mathrm{avg}}=\dfrac{F}{A_{\mathrm{shear}}}\)，\(\sigma_{bs}=\dfrac{F}{A_{\mathrm{bearing}}}\)

4. 弯曲、截面、组合应力

  • \(\sigma=\dfrac{My}{I}\)
  • \(\sigma=\dfrac{N}{A}\pm\dfrac{My}{I}\)
  • \(I=I_c+Ad^2\)
  • \(|\sigma|_{\max}\le[\sigma]\)

5. 应力状态、莫尔圆、强度理论

  • \(\bar{\sigma}=\dfrac{\sigma_x+\sigma_y}{2}\)
  • \(R=\sqrt{\left(\dfrac{\sigma_x-\sigma_y}{2}\right)^2+\tau_{xy}^2}\)
  • \(\sigma_{1,2}=\bar{\sigma}\pm R\)，\(\tau_{\max}=R\)
  • \(\varepsilon_x=\dfrac{\sigma_x-\nu(\sigma_y+\sigma_z)}{E}\)，\(\gamma_{xy}=\dfrac{\tau_{xy}}{G}\)
  • \(G=\dfrac{E}{2(1+\nu)}\)
  • \(\sigma_{eq}^{T}=\max\left(|\sigma_1-\sigma_2|,\ |\sigma_2-\sigma_3|,\ |\sigma_3-\sigma_1|\right)\)
  • \(\sigma_{eq}^{M}=\sqrt{\dfrac{(\sigma_1-\sigma_2)^2+(\sigma_2-\sigma_3)^2+(\sigma_3-\sigma_1)^2}{2}}\)

6. 位移、能量法、卡氏定理

  • \(EI\,v''(x)=M(x)\)
  • \(U=\int\dfrac{N^2}{2EA}\,dx+\int\dfrac{T^2}{2GJ}\,dx+\int\dfrac{M^2}{2EI}\,dx\)
  • \(\delta_i=\dfrac{\partial U}{\partial P_i}\)，\(\theta_i=\dfrac{\partial U}{\partial M_i}\)

7. 稳定

  • \(P_{cr}=\dfrac{\pi^2EI}{(\mu l)^2}\)
  • \(\lambda=\dfrac{\mu l}{i}\)，\(\sigma_{cr}=\dfrac{\pi^2E}{\lambda^2}\)
  • 两端铰支 \(\mu=1\)，一端固定一端自由 \(\mu=2\)，一端固定一端铰支 \(\mu\approx0.7\)，两端固定 \(\mu=0.5\)
做综合题时，最后要比较强度、刚度、稳定条件，取最不利的那个。
